Stories of Love

Behind every celebration, there's a love story waiting to bloom. Here, we share the beautiful journeys of our couples—stories of romance, artistry, and unforgettable moments.

April 2015 Lagos Botanical Gardens

Amara & Chidi

A Garden Romance

When Amara and Chidi first met at a friend's garden party, they knew their wedding would need to honor that special place where it all began. Under a canopy of blooms and soft natural light, their love story unfolded in the most magical way. Every detail reflected their journey — from the delicate ivory petals that framed the ceremony arch to the flowing silhouette of Amara's custom gown. Her bridal look was a perfect blend of classic romance and modern elegance, featuring hand-embroidered lace that caught the sunlight as she walked down the garden aisle.

"3SEVENS transformed our vision into something beyond our wildest dreams. From the first consultation to the final moment, every detail was handled with such care and artistry. Amara looked absolutely breathtaking, and the florals took our breath away."

— Amara, Lagos Botanical Gardens.

March 2025 Transcorp Hilton, Abuja

Zainab & Ibrahim

Timeless Elegance

Zainab and Ibrahim's celebration was the epitome of refined luxury and cultural richness. Every detail reflected their sophisticated taste and the depth of their connection. From the intricate lace details of Zainab's gown to the cascading floral installations that transformed the ballroom, their wedding was a testament to enduring love and timeless beauty. The fusion of traditional elements with contemporary elegance created an unforgettable atmosphere that honored both their heritage and their modern love story.

"Working with 3SEVENS was an absolute dream. They understood our vision for a grand yet intimate celebration. Zainab has never looked more beautiful, and our guests are still talking about the stunning floral arrangements months later."

— Zainab, Transcorp Hilton, Abuja.

February 2025 Lekki Conservation Centre

Ngozi & Tunde

Surrounded by lush florals and natural beauty, Ngozi and Tunde celebrated love in its purest form. As nature enthusiasts who met on a hiking trail, they wanted their wedding to reflect their love for the outdoors and organic beauty. The ceremony took place beneath towering trees, with the natural canopy providing shade and ambiance. The organic elegance of their wedding design captured the essence of their gentle, authentic romance, with every element thoughtfully chosen to complement the natural surroundings.

"3SEVENS brought our botanical dream to life in ways we couldn't have imagined. They respected our desire for natural beauty while adding touches of elegance that made everything feel so special. Ngozi felt like a forest goddess, and the florals were absolutely magical."

— Ngozi, Lekki Conservation Centre.
