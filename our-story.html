A Story of Elegance, Craft, and Celebration

Where passion meets artistry, and every detail tells a story of love

Born from Passion, Crafted with Love

3Sevens was born from a deep love for artistry, emotion, and the beauty of life's most precious moments. We believe that every celebration deserves to be extraordinary — where every stitch tells a story, every bloom whispers romance, and every detail reflects the unique essence of love. Based in Nigeria, our creative studio blends timeless bridal elegance with breathtaking floral design, creating experiences that transcend the ordinary. We don't just design dresses or arrange flowers — we craft memories, shape dreams, and celebrate individuality with uncompromising attention to detail. From the first sketch to the final reveal, we pour our hearts into every creation, ensuring that your most important day reflects not just beauty, but the profound joy and meaning it holds.

Our Vision

Redefine bridal and floral artistry through storytelling and design. Each project is an opportunity to create something personal that holds meaning.

Our Philosophy

We translate emotions. At 3Sevens, beauty is intentional. Each detail is guided by care and craftsmanship. Our work believes perfection lives in authenticity and artistry in passion.

Our Creative Spirit

At the heart of 3Sevens is a team of passionate creatives, seamstresses, designers, and floral artists who share one vision: to transform dreams into tangible beauty. We are storytellers with fabric and flowers. We are perfectionists who see magic in the smallest details. We are dreamers who believe that love deserves to be celebrated in the most exquisite way possible. Every gown is sewn with intention. Every bouquet is arranged with care. Every celebration we touch becomes a work of art — because we don't just create for you, we create with you. This is more than a business. It's a calling. And we are honored to be part of your journey.
